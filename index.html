Research & Development Projects

HugMeter

HugMeter is a "low-pressure" social tool designed to bridge the gap between digital communication and physical affection. The application allows users to send virtual hugs and track the physical distance to their loved ones, facilitating emotional connection without the demand of a full conversation. Built with a privacy-first architecture, it explores how approximate location data can be leveraged to foster interpersonal closeness.

StockMeter

StockMeter is a monitoring tool developed for educational and research purposes, facilitating the tracking of global market indices, mutual funds, and equities. The project explores the integration of mobile applications with private cloud backends, utilizing a Zero-Knowledge Architecture. By leveraging the user's personal Google Drive for data storage, the application ensures that financial intelligence and portfolio strategies remain entirely decentralized and private.
